Smartcities


The Smart cities or intelligent cities, they are the result of the need increasingly imperious
of orientating our life towards the sustainability. This way, these cities are served infrastructures,
innovation and technology to diminish the energetic consumption and to reduce the CO2 emissions.

Of what do these measures consist towards the sustainability?


To be served photovoltaic panels in the communities,
more means of transport and electrical vehicles, wind mills in lampposts, solar panels for semaphores or signs,
promotion and development of the use of bicycles … The Smart Cities uses variety of resources, and all these small
acts lead to a city to turning in more efficient and sustainable.
